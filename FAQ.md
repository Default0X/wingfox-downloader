# FAQ

## What is this tool for?

Wingfox Downloader helps authorized Wingfox users save local copies of their course videos.

Typical reasons include unstable internet, difficulty streaming long videos, or wanting to study offline from courses the user already owns or can legally access.

## Can I share downloaded courses with other people?

No.

Downloaded Wingfox course files are for your own authorized personal use only. Do not redistribute, upload, sell, mirror, lend, or share downloaded course videos or course materials.

Wingfox's User Services Agreement restricts copying, recording, selling, account sharing, and commercial use of its service without written consent. It also states that improper account use, including multiple people using the same account and reproduction-related violations, can result in action against the account.

Reference: https://www.wingfox.com/agree

## Can I use this with courses I do not own?

No.

Only use it with courses you own, purchased, subscribed to, or otherwise have permission to access through your Wingfox account.

## Does this tool bypass Wingfox login?

No.

You must log in through the browser when the downloader asks you to. The tool depends on your own authorized session.

## Where are downloads saved?

Downloads are saved in the `Downloads` folder beside the application.

## The download failed. What should I do?

1. Confirm that you are logged in to Wingfox.
2. Confirm that the course page opens normally in your browser.
3. Keep the extracted folder structure intact.
4. Try running the downloader again.
5. If the issue continues, open a bug report using the instructions in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
